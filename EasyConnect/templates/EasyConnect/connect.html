Services
The service provided by EasyConnectMD is for acute, urgent care type
visits. Anxiety and depression cannot be diagnosed or treated.
Prescriptions
Prescriptions for controlled substances for non-therapeutic use, and/or those
which may be harmful (potential for abuse or addiction) as well as general
medicine refills (for chronic conditions) will not be written here.
Refunds
Refunds will not be processed because we were unable to diagnose or treat you
nor because a prescription was unable to be filled due to medical restrictions.
We will however issue a refund if you are unable to connect with a member of
our staff.
