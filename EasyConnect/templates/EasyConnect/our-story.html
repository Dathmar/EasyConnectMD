About KellumConnectMD.com Virtual Weight Loss Clinic

The KellumConnectMD.com Virtual Weight Loss Clinic has been established to serve as a tool for Texans to work on weight loss issues. This virtual weight loss clinic is the evolution of the telemedicine program originated by Dr. Daniel H. Kellum Jr. MD, a Board-Certified Family Practice physician.

Powered by Kellum Family Medicine, a medical practice rooted in the San Antonio Texas Community for over 40 years, the KellumConnectMD.com Virtual Weight Loss Clinic will specialize in long term preventive and proactive care. This virtual clinic was established as a platform to provide a low cost and effective way for our patients to battle weight loss by utilizing secure telemedicine technology along with the most innovative weight loss medications and treatments available.
