About EasyConnectMD.com

EasyConnectMD is the evolution of the telemedicine program originated at Kellum Family Medicine in San Antonio Texas. Dr. Daniel H. Kellum Jr. MD, a Board Certified Family Practice physician, is the co-owner and medical director who led the establishment of the EasyConnectMD platform in an effort to offer quality health care at an affordable cost to communities throughout the State of Texas. To date, EasyConnectMD has served every major City and quadrant in the State of Texas from El Paso to Houston to Dallas to the Rio Grande Valley. EasyConnectMD is powered by Kellum Family Medicine and has fully licensed medical providers working throughout the State of Texas.

Why Visit EasyConnectMD.com?

Telemedicine provides patients who are feeling bad and in need of non-emergency medical attention or information an opportunity to see a provider from their home, office or any place where they can use a phone, tablet or computer and there is a good internet connection.
